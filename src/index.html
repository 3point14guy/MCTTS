Presentations can range from geographic, cultural, political, etc. to facts about the country. Participants can recite a poem, tell a folk tale, present a skit, create an interactive game, sing or dance or ellicit audience participation, or anything else the students would like to share; the more creative, the better. Group presentations are welcome. We often get to hear from a guest speaker who lived or travelled extensively in the country we are studying.


Presentations are for home schoolers ages 7 and above. Younger siblings are welcome to watch the presentatipons or participate in an older siblings presentation.









The Asheville Homeschool Culture Club is open to any family. Space is limited and enrollment in the club is contigent upon approval and payment of annual dues. If we reach capacity for our current facilities, we will gladly put you on a wait list.


The annual dues for AshHCC are only $18 per child and cover costs of our facility rental and supplies. In addition to dues, parents assist with set up, clean up and contributing to the pot luck. Families bring authentic food from the country of study to share. Please be sure to bring sufficient quantity of your dish so that everyone may get a helping.
